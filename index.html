I am a research scientist at PROFACTOR / JKU, working at the intersection of computer vision and robotics. My research interests are in fusing deterministic machine learning with probabilistic models via stochastic computational graphs and graph neural networks.
In the past, I developed a popular real-time 3D reconstruction software named ReconstructMe and initiated several open source projects. These are now used by a variety of research groups worldwide, including Facebook, Google, and Microsoft.
Interests. Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Robotics.
